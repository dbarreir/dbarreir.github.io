Page design

  • Home page: The purpose is to attract the attention of the clients. The audience will be interested clients or general visitors. The content of the page will be an attractive image to capture clients, with services and a contact form. Yes, I'm asking for name, email and the message to contact the owner. Yes, there will be email validation. There will be a button for the form and hyperlinks on the nav. When the client clicks the button a message of form sent succesfully will be displayed and will mantain position of page.
  • Services page: The purpose of this page is to let know the clients which services the business provides. The audience will be interested clients. The content of the page will be a extended version of details of the services offered from the client. No, there will be no asked data on this page. No validation needed. Yes, there will be hyperlinks on the nav. This hyperlinks will take you to the desired page.
  • Protfolio page: The purpose of this page is to showcase to the clients the services provided with images. The audience will be interest clients. The content of the page will be a showcase of images of the services provided. No, there will be no asked data on this page. No validation needed. Yes, there will be hyperlinks on the nav. This hyperlinks will take you to the desired page.
  • About page: The purpose of this page is to let the clients know a bit more about the client. The audience will be interested clients. The content of this page will be a bio and maybe an image of the owner. No, there will be no asked data on this page. No validation needed. Yes, there will be hyperlinks on the nav. This hyperlinks will take you to the desired page.
  • FAQ page: The purpose of this page is to help clients with frequented questions they may have. The audience will be interested clients. The content of this page will be multiples accordions that will hide/show the selected information. No, there will be no asked data on this page. No validation needed. Yes, there will be hyperlinks on the nav and also a button to show/hide accordions. This hyperlinks will take you to the desired page and the buttons show you the desired accordion information.

Dynamic functionality

I'll use javascript on the form to validate email and to show a confirmation or error message when trying to submit the form. I'll also use javascript to make the accordion functionality smoother.
